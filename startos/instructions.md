# Hashboard

Open **Hashboard** from your StartOS services and launch the Web UI (LAN).

1. Open the UI on your phone (same network as your miners).
2. Miner switcher -> **Scan LAN** to discover miners (BraiinsOS+ / CGMiner API, port 4028).
3. Tap a miner for live hashrate, power and temperature.
4. Use the power slider to set the target; Pause/Resume to start/stop.

**Network:** the StartOS node must be on the same LAN as your miners.
